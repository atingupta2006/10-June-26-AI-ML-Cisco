# Team sales (Day 1)

`team_sales.csv` — quarterly sales for 100 city/product teams (4 US regions).

Used in Day 1 Labs 3, 4, and 6 (Excel and/or Jupyter).

`lab_profile.json` — expected numbers for notebook checks (do not edit).

Columns: `team`, `team_name`, `region`, `q1_sales`, `q2_sales`, plus product and unit fields.
