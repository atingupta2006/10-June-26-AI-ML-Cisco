# Runbook snippets for RAG labs (synthetic)

## VPN tunnel drop (IOS-XE)
Verify IKE phase 1/2, check peer reachability, compare crypto map. Clear SA with clear crypto sa if policy allows.

## BGP peering (NX-OS)
Check neighbor state, AS number, prefix limits. Review last notification and hold-timer.

## Interface flapping
Collect show interface counters; look for physical vs err-disabled; check STP topology changes.

## E-404 router error
Often config drift on interface VLAN. Compare running vs golden config; rollback last change.

## Security alert port_scan
Isolate source VLAN, enable ACL deny, open SOC ticket with five-tuple and timeframe.

## UCS HA failover
Validate fabric interconnect heartbeat; confirm service profile placement; check SAN connectivity.
