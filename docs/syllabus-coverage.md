# Syllabus coverage

Maps course topics to hands-on labs. Primary data: `data/support-ops/` (`support_tickets.csv`, `network_events.csv`, `router_logs.csv`, `security_logs.csv`, `kb_snippets.txt`).

## Topics

- **Gen AI** — Day 1 introduction; Days 3–5 prompts and agents
- **Prompt Engineering** — Day 3 extract/compare; Day 4 patterns and TAC drafts
- **Hugging Face** — Day 3 ticket summarize; Day 4 router/security inference
- **RAG** — Day 5 KB chunking, Q&A, ticket history
- **MCP** — Day 5 mock tools lab
- **Agentic AI** — Day 5 triage and error-spike cases; Day 6 capstone
- **Ticket Analysis** — Days 1–3 ticket counts and trends; Day 6 reports
- **Log Analysis** — Day 2 security/router; Day 4 HF on logs
- **Anomaly Detection** — Day 6 security exploration and rare alerts
- **Python** — Day 2 structures and pandas workflows

Six training days; six labs per day under `hands-on/`.
