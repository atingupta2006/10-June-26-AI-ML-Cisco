# Course overview

Six days of hands-on labs. Each day has six Jupyter notebooks under `hands-on/<day>/notebooks/`.

| Day | Topic | Dataset |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 1 | Data science basics, stats, Excel group work | Team sales (100 rows) |
| 2 | Python, NumPy, Pandas, plots, linear regression | Zomato restaurants |
| 3 | Classification, metrics, pipelines, SHAP | Lending Club sample |
| 4 | Distance metrics, KNN, FastAPI, MLflow | Lending Club sample |
| 5 | Clustering and evaluation | NYSE stocks |
| 6 | Outliers, imbalance, fraud detection | Credit card transactions |

Official syllabus text is in [course-content.txt](../course-content.txt) at the training workspace root (your instructor may share a copy).

Start with [student quick start](student-quick-start.md) and the [lab execution guide](lab-execution-guide.md).
